Privacy Policy

Mis Gastos / My Expenses
Last updated: March 13, 2026

1. Overview

Mis Gastos / My Expenses is a free, offline personal finance application developed by RSVF RoSDev. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All information you enter in the app — expenses, incomes, recurring payments, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (SQLite database and SharedPreferences). No data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests. No data is sent to or received from any server.

4. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Storage & Security

All app data resides solely on your device. You can delete all data at any time by uninstalling the app. We have no access to your data.

6. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: rsvf99@gmail.com
